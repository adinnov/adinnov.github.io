About A.D. Innovations LLC

A.D. Innovations LLC is a veteran-owned cybersecurity company founded to deliver exceptional cyber training and operations support across federal, educational, and commercial sectors. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations with high-impact knowledge, drawn from real-world experience in national defense, education, and cybersecurity engineering.

Founder Background

A.D. Innovations LLC was founded by Andrew De La Rosa, a cybersecurity professional with a decade of experience supporting mission-critical systems across military, intelligence, education, and private sector environments. Andrew holds an M.S. and B.S. in Computer Engineering from Florida International University, and is certified in Security+, Scrum Master (CSM), and as a Registered DoD RMF Practitioner.

Career Highlights

  • Cyber Analyst for U.S. Army tactical readiness and training systems under COLSA Corporation
  • Cybersecurity lead for Lockheed Martin’s F-35 and international defense programs including Navy and NATO efforts
  • Cybersecurity instructor for Columbia University and Director of Cyber-Zeb I.T. Training Academy
  • Expert in RMF, STIG compliance, SIEM deployment, network hardening, DevSecOps, and secure architecture

Andrew’s journey spans across roles from Cyber Intel Analyst to ISSO and Cybersecurity Instructor, with hands-on contributions to cutting-edge 5G AR/VR development, secure satellite infrastructure, and defense simulation platforms.

Our Vision

At A.D. Innovations LLC, we believe cybersecurity readiness is a shared responsibility and a vital foundation for resilience in any organization. Whether you're part of a city municipality, a federal agency, a university, or a private enterprise, we’re here to equip you with knowledge, tools, and strategies that matter.

Our approach is rooted in mentorship, technical depth, and real-world application — a formula built to meet today’s threats head-on and prepare tomorrow’s cyber leaders.
